To enable machines to acquire a similar ability, we propose a two-stage framework for predicting emotions based on body language from regular RGB video inputs. In the first stage, the model predicts body language from input videos by estimating human poses. The predicted body language is then fed into the second stage for emotion interpretation. We define body language as either a maintained posture or a period of repeated short actions. Related research has suggested the recognition of various activities, such as standing/sitting, normal walking, running, and jogging, on smartphone devices. This research proposed an efficient combination of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks (an improvement over Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN)), and a self-attention model to represent activity features. During the data collection phase, it is assumed that smartphone usage is unconstrained, and inertial sensors, including acceleration, gyroscope, and linear acceleration sensors, are employed.

Problem Statement:

Traditional CCTV surveillance systems rely heavily on human operators for monitoring, which is labour-intensive, prone to human error, and inefficient in identifying suspicious activities. The need for continuous attention makes it difficult to detect and respond to potential threats promptly. Consequently, there is an urgent need for an intelligent, autonomous surveillance system that can accurately detect suspicious human behavior and predict potential malicious actions in real-time, thereby enhancing security and preventing crimes more effectively. This project addresses these limitations by leveraging AI technologies to create a more efficient and reliable and Scalable surveillance solution.

Objectives Of The Project:

This project aims to develop an advanced AI-driven surveillance system capable of detecting suspicious behavior autonomously, predicting actions based on behavioral analysis, ensuring real-time monitoring for immediate response, minimizing false alarms to enhance reliability, complying with privacy regulations, and designing for scalability and seamless integration with existing infrastructure.

  • Develop an Intelligent Surveillance System: Focuses on developing an advanced CCTV system enhanced with artificial intelligence to autonomously detect suspicious human behavior and predict subsequent actions.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: This objective ensures that the surveillance system operates seamlessly in real-time, providing instant alerts to security personnel.
  • Minimize False Positives: This aims to enhance the accuracy of the system by reducing false alarms and improving reliability.
  • Compliance and Privacy: This objective ensures that AI systems are developed, deployed, and used in ways that are lawful, ethical, and respectful of individual's rights and freedoms.
  • Scalability and Integration: This ensure that the solution is scalable and can seamlessly integrate with existing surveillance infrastructure.

These objectives collectively aim to develop an advanced AI-driven surveillance system that enhances security effectiveness, operates in real-time, and integrates smoothly with existing infrastructure.

Conclusion And Future Work:

We anticipate that our advanced CCTV system will significantly enhance security by providing real-time, autonomous detection and prediction of suspicious human behavior and actions with high accuracy and fewer false alarms. Our goal is to deliver a scalable solution that not only improves public safety but also offers a robust framework for safeguarding communities effectively.

  • Refine AI Models: We plan to improve our algorithms to better adapt to diverse environmental conditions. This will involve fine-tuning our models to handle various lighting, weather conditions, and different types of movement, ensuring that the system remains effective in all scenarios.
  • Boost Real: Time Processing- To minimize processing delays, we aim to develop low-latency algorithms. This will enhance the system's ability to process video feeds quickly, enabling faster detection and response to potential threats.
  • Web Application Development: We intend to create a user-friendly web application that will facilitate real-time monitoring and analysis. This interface will provide users with intuitive controls and comprehensive analytics to make it easier to manage and interpret surveillance data.
  • Adaptive Learning: Our system will incorporate mechanisms for continuous learning and adaptation. By regularly updating and enhancing AI models based on new data and insights, we will ensure that the system remains effective and up-to-date with evolving security needs and emerging threats.
